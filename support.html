Find your direction


Upon opening the mobile application, locate the "Direction" tab beneath the map. Click on it to reveal the path to your destination.


Choose Different Floors


On the left side of the map, you'll find a set of numbers. These represent different floors. Click on a number to switch between floors and explore various levels of the map.


Exploration Tab


There's an "Exploration" on the tab's left side. Click on it to activate a feature that will focus on the room you've searched.
